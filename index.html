Tripista Privacy Policy

Thank you for choosing Tripista, a travel planning app designed to enhance your trip planning experience. This Privacy Policy outlines how your personal information is collected, used, and stored within the app. Your privacy is of utmost importance to us, and we are committed to protecting it.

1. Information We Collect

- User-Provided Information: User-Provided Information: When you register for Tripista, we collect your name, email address, password, and profile picture.

- Storage Permission: Tripista requests storage permission to save your trip plans and related data locally on your device.

- Camera Permission: If you choose to take photos directly from the camera, Tripista requests camera permission to facilitate this feature.

- Contact Permission: Tripista may request contact permission for enhancing user experience, but please note that your contact data is not transferred to any third party.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

- Personalize your experience within the app.

- Save and organize your trip plans locally on your device.

- Enable the camera feature to capture and store photos for your trips.

3. Third-Party Services

Tripista does not transfer your personal information to any third party. All data is stored locally on your device.

4. Read/Write Storage

Tripista utilizes read and write permissions for storage to ensure seamless functionality. This allows the app to retrieve and update your trip information stored locally on your device.

5. Account Deletion

You have the option to delete your Tripista account at any time. When you delete your account, all associated personal information will be permanently removed from our servers. Please note that this action is irreversible.

6. Security

Your data security is our priority. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access and disclosure.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify this privacy policy at any time, so please review it frequently. Changes and clarifications will take effect immediately upon posting on the app.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at wazeenp@gmail.com. We are here to assist you and address any privacy-related inquiries you may have.

By using Tripista, you agree to the terms outlined in this privacy policy.
